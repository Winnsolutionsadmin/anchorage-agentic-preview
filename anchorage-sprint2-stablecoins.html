The sprint cadence (one subject, one week)

The paper is the payload; the interviews are the distribution. One stablecoin subject (the neutrality thesis, the settlement rail for agents, or a policy moment) cascades into a research paper, a Nathan video, and a partner interview, run video-first and laddered into the AI conversation.

Lead with neutrality, never a single issuer. Any backing, market-share, issuer-naming, partner-name, or volume claim is COUNSEL and is gated until cleared.

The Voice

Neutrality, in his own words and ours

The belief is Nathan's; the drafts are ours, gated on the voice spine. We never invent a position he has not stated.

“Increased neutrality, not pushing any one stablecoin.”

Nathan McCauley · the neutrality thesis

The register stays neutral and institutional: lead with the principle that a regulated bank can hold the neutral position no single issuer can, then let the settlement-rail-for-agents thesis carry the AI ladder.

Draft tweet-run themes (gated on voice spine plus compliance)
  • The issuer war is a race to the bottom. Neutrality is the position a regulated bank gets to hold.
  • Agents settle in dollars that never sleep. The question is whose dollars, and under whose rules.
  • Regulated issuance is what turns a stablecoin into agent-grade money: auditable, accountable, neutral.
Goals & KPIs

Three goals for Sprint 2

What the sprint is measured against, carried from the campaign-level goals.

  • Own neutrality as the position

    KPI: the neutrality frame quoted back externally, and tier-1 coverage that uses it rather than an issuer story.

  • Earn share of voice in the AI conversation

    KPI: reach and share of voice on the settlement-rail-for-agents theme, video-first content above the baseline.

  • Prove the institutional register without overclaiming

    KPI: institutional-audience engagement, zero backing or volume overclaims (the honesty KPI).

The Gates

What is clean, what routes to counsel

Clean (principle, voice review)

The neutrality principle, the settlement-rail-for-agents category, and the macro research framing are CLEAN in Nathan's voice, subject to voice review.

Counsel (route before any external use)

Any backing, reserve, market-share, issuer-naming, partner-name, or volume claim, and any "Anchorage issues X" capability, is COUNSEL. No Nathan-voice content ships before the voice spine reaches v1.0 and compliance signs off.

The bottom line

Neutral, regulated, and built for agents. That is the rail worth owning.
