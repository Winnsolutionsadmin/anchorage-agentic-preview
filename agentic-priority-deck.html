Don't sell the labor saved. Sell the revenue retained.

The consulting wedge that moves agentic AI from a line-item pilot to a board-level re-platform.

10 · Bridge · consumer · marketplace verdictSequenced re-skins

One trust asset, extended three times.

The institutional bridge leads. Consumer and marketplace are sequenced re-skins, not separate builds.

The bridge · GTM

The institutional motion is a cross-sell of a second product onto an existing custody / settlement relationship, not cold acquisition ("the relationship is the rail," already live with two named clients). Sequence: funds / custody, then fintechs / PSPs, then agent-builders / L1s, then banks, then prestige / data.

Consumer · do both, sequenced

Embedder-first (B2B2C) leads, an embeddable get-agentic-wallet SDK behind someone else's brand; direct-to-consumer follows once the surface is proven. Tease the principle (the chartered "trust fall" entity), never the product, until the flow exists.

Marketplace · build the trust interface, cede the catalog

Cede the catalog / checkout UI (saturated: Google UCP / AP2, OpenAI / Stripe ACP, Visa, Circle). Build the verified-both-sides trust interface (KYA'd buyer, KYB'd merchant, regulated perimeter, human recourse). Be the trust the marketplaces route through, not a marketplace. Gate hard until one verified reference transaction exists.
