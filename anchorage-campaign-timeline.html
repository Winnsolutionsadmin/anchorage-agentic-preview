The Cadence

How the sprints overlap

The machine oils up: prep for the next sprint begins around day ten of the current one, so launches land roughly monthly without a cold start each time.

  • Three to four weeks prep

    Research paper drafted and source-checked, video and tweets drafted to the voice spine, partner booked, assets built.

  • One week launch

    The position publishes, the stack fires the same hour, the cascade opens.

  • Traction and amplify follow

    First numbers, clips, partner reshares, recap, feeding the next sprint.

Every date is gated

No launch ships ahead of demoable substance, and no Nathan-voice content ships before the voice spine reaches v1.0 and compliance signs off. The calendar is a plan, not a commitment to publish on a fixed day.
