The discipline

Tease the category, gate the capability. The enabling-layer category and the welcome-rivals reframe are real and tease-eligible; any sub-custody capability, named bank, onboarding claim, or volume number is COUNSEL and stays gated until demoable.

The Voice

Welcome rivals, in his own words and ours

The belief is Nathan's; the drafts are ours, gated on the voice spine.

“There are now nine crypto banks. What I want is 4,000.”

Nathan McCauley · plurality and the enabling layer

The register is generous and confident: a chartered bank rooting for rivals reads as strength, not weakness, and it is the on-brand way to claim the enabling layer the whole field would build on.

Draft tweet-run themes (gated on voice spine plus compliance)
  • Every bank becomes an AI bank, the way every company became an internet company. The question is what they build on.
  • We do not want to be the one bank. We want to be the layer the other 3,999 build on.
  • The demand is already here. What is missing is a chartered floor institutions can plug their agents into.
Goals & KPIs

Three goals for Sprint 4

  • Own the bank-for-banks demand engine

    KPI: the enabling-layer framing quoted back, and coverage that reads Anchorage as the layer others build on.

  • Convert the welcome-rivals reframe into inbound

    KPI: institution and partner inbound referencing sub-custody or the enabling layer by concept.

  • Prove it without overclaiming

    KPI: zero named-bank or capability overclaims; sub-custody referenced as concept only (the honesty KPI).

The Gates

What is clean, what routes to counsel

Clean (principle, voice review)

The enabling-layer category, the welcome-rivals reframe, and the macro research framing are CLEAN in Nathan's voice, subject to voice review.

Counsel (route before any external use)

Any sub-custody capability, named-bank, onboarding, client-count, or volume claim is COUNSEL. No Nathan-voice content ships before the voice spine reaches v1.0 and compliance signs off.

The bottom line

The category grows when everyone is in it. Be the floor they all stand on.
