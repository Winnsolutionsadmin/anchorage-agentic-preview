Ribbit partner track

Carl's intel: Ribbit Capital is building in the agentic-identity space. The play is a co-sign that borrows Ribbit's portfolio audience (the consumer reach Anchorage does not natively have) through a livestream, a fireside, and a co-authored piece.

Research paper integration

"Who Banks the Agents?" is already written (~3,800 words, evidence-backed). It is the credibility engine of the sprint. It must clear compliance before external chop-up; pre-print teases run sooner.

Waitlist funnel & landing page

The capture layer. The landing page gets a waitlist signup. The framing lets people read between the lines (everybody will want a bank account for their agent) while making no claim that a consumer agent account is shipping.
