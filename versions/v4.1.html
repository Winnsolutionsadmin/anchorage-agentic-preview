Drip to funnel

The sprint is shaped as a drip that resolves into one on-ramp. It is not three identical stages: a wide tease seeds the question for days, the reveal and proof concentrate the argument, and everything narrows to a single waitlist door.

Stage 01 · the long drip
Seed the question, then keep asking it.
All-caps breadcrumbs (WHO BANKS THE AGENTS, KYA, PROOF OF AGENT) open the field for days with no context. The CEO and the brand handle ask the same four words in one voice, so it reads as a staked position. KOLs react in public, which turns a slogan into a conversation.
runs Jun 22 → ongoing  ·  widest top of funnel
02
Stage 02 · concentrate
Resolve it into a thesis.
The raw selfie reveal, the research paper, and the Ribbit / Malka co-sign turn the tease into a citable argument with named backers. Proof week shows the controls: allowance not wallet, the 3am test, agents run internally.
03
Stage 03 · one door
Route everything to the waitlist.
Press breaks the story, the waitlist opens, and the live fireside airs. Every thread, KOL boost, partner post, and the paper points to one agentic landing page and one signup, framed as a builder signal to lower the bar and raise intent.
End state: every breadcrumb, paper post, partner co-sign, and press hit routes to the agentic landing page and the Agentic Banking waitlist. The waitlist is the single conversion event the whole sprint is built to produce.
