Agentic GTM Execution Schedule

The whole agentic go-to-market rollout on one time axis: the 8-phase launch sprint (P1 tease through P8 amplify), the eighteen workstreams sequenced by priority and dependency, and the no-input items that can move now. Weeks W0 to W12.

Dates are estimates

Every position on this axis is a planning estimate, not a committed date. The real clock starts when Anchorage sets the launch date and the gating preconditions clear: VOICE_SPINE v1.0 + compliance sign-off (blocks all Nathan-voice copy), the load-bearing stat source-check (blocks the research paper), and the bot guardrail review (blocks bot launch). W0 anchors to "launch hour"; weeks before it are the T-14 pre-work runway.

How to read this. Band A is the launch sprint compressing P1–P6 into launch hour (W0), with the talent/waitlist/bot parallel lane spanning it and P7/P8 as the immediate follow-on; the T-14→T-3 pre-work runway precedes W0 and is gated by the voice spine, stat source-check, and bot guardrail review. Band C is the work that needs no Anchorage decision and can move now. Band B places the eighteen workstreams: WS-01 and WS-02 are executing now as the subtraction-gate foundation, the H items (WS-03 account engine, WS-04 sandbox, WS-05 cards) sequence next, the M items follow their dependencies, and the L items are instrumented now but productized on the 2027 horizon.

Estimate discipline. No bar is a committed date. Positions shift the moment Anchorage sets the launch clock and the voice-spine / compliance gates clear. The gated P5 demo may slip out of the sprint entirely, which is by design: the sprint launches the category without it.
